Devices

Repair Devices

We repair a wide range of electronic devices, including:

Caravan Motor Movers & PSU Charger Repairs

We repair caravan and motorhome power supply units, battery chargers and control modules. Postal repair available UK-wide.

Audio Equipment Repairs

We repair speakers, amplifiers, mixers and PA systems including Bose, Sony, QSC, ZLX and more.

Hi-Fi & High-End Audio Repairs

We repair amplifiers, receivers, DACs, speakers, subwoofers and Hi-Fi separates. Experienced with Cambridge Audio, Tannoy, Wharfedale, DALI, Marantz, Denon, NAD, Arcam, KEF and many others.

Sony Shortwave Radio Repairs

Professional repair service for Sony ICF-SW55, ICF-SW77, ICF-SW100, ICF-PRO80 and ICF-SW1 shortwave radios. We replace leaking capacitors, repair power and audio faults, and upgrade failed LCD backlights to energy-efficient LEDs. Postal repairs available throughout the UK.
