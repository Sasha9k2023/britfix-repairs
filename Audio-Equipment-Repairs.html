Audio Equipment Repairs

Expert repairs for amplifiers, speakers, mixers, PA systems and professional audio equipment

At Britfix Repairs, we specialise in audio equipment repairs at component level. Whether your amplifier has no power, your speaker has distorted sound, or your PA system has stopped working — we can help.

  • Amplifier, speaker, mixer and PA system repairs
  • Power supply faults, blown output stages and protection mode faults
  • Repairs for Bose, Velodyne DD, Sony,QSC K8 K10 K12.2 KS212C KLA12, EV ZLX 12P/15P and many other brands
  • UK-wide tracked mail-in service available

Common Issues We Fix

  • ⛔ No power or dead unit
  • 🔇 No sound or very low output
  • ⚠️ Distorted sound or crackling
  • 🔥 Burning smell or short circuit
  • 🔁 Protection mode fault
  • 🔌 Power supply failure

We help customers across the UK repair audio equipment without replacing the whole system. Contact us directly to get started.
