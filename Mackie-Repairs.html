Mackie Active Loudspeaker Repairs UK

Professional repair service for Mackie active loudspeakers, powered speakers and PA systems throughout the UK.

At Britfix Repairs, we specialise in component-level repairs for Mackie active loudspeakers. We repair amplifier modules, switch-mode power supplies, DSP boards, protection circuits and damaged audio stages.

  • Mackie SRM Series
  • Mackie Thump Series
  • Mackie DRM Series
  • Mackie HD Series
  • UK tracked postal repair service

Common Faults We Repair

  • 🔴 No power
  • 🔊 Distorted or weak audio
  • ⚠ Protection mode
  • 🔥 Blown amplifier output stage
  • ⚡ Power supply failure
  • 🔇 No sound
  • 🎛 DSP faults
  • 🔌 Damaged input circuits

We repair most Mackie active loudspeakers without replacing the complete amplifier module, saving customers the cost of expensive replacement parts.
