FBT Active Speaker Repairs UK

Professional repair service for FBT active loudspeakers, powered speakers and PA systems throughout the UK.

At Britfix Repairs, we specialise in component-level repairs for FBT active loudspeakers. We repair amplifier modules, switch-mode power supplies, DSP boards, protection circuits and damaged audio stages.

Where possible, we repair the original amplifier module rather than replacing the complete assembly, helping customers avoid the high cost of replacement parts.

FBT Speaker Models We Repair

  • FBT X-Lite Series
  • FBT J Series
  • FBT ProMaxX Series
  • FBT Vertus Series
  • Other FBT powered speakers and active PA systems
  • UK tracked postal repair service

Common Faults We Repair

  • 🔴 No power or completely dead speaker
  • 🔊 Distorted, weak or intermittent audio
  • ⚠ Protection mode faults
  • 🔥 Blown amplifier output stage
  • ⚡ Switch-mode power supply failure
  • 🔇 No sound from the loudspeaker
  • 🛠 DSP board and control faults
  • 🔌 Damaged input or output connections
  • 🌡 Speaker shutting down when warm
  • 💡 Display, indicator or control-panel faults

Component-Level FBT Repairs

Our repairs may include replacement of failed power supply components, amplifier transistors, capacitors, regulators, protection components, connectors and damaged circuit-board tracks.

Every speaker is inspected before repair work begins. Once the fault has been identified, we provide a repair estimate before proceeding.

Customers throughout the UK can send their FBT speaker or amplifier module to us using a tracked delivery service.
