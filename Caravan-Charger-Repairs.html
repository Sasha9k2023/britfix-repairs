Caravan and Motorhome Charger Repairs

Professional repairs for caravan power supplies, battery chargers and leisure battery charging systems throughout the UK.

At Britfix Repairs, we specialise in component-level caravan charger and PSU repairs. We repair charging units, power supplies, transformers, control circuits and damaged electronic components where possible.

Common faults include no charging output, incorrect battery voltage, blown fuses, intermittent operation, overheating, damaged power components and complete power failure.

Chargers and Power Units We Repair

  • Powerpart P0120 12V 20A Charger PSU Repairs
  • Powerpart P0123 Three-Stage Charger Repairs
  • Caravan 10A Power Unit and Transformer Repairs
  • Zig Charger Repairs
  • Leisure Battery Charging Faults

Common Charger Faults We Repair

  • 🔴 No power or completely dead unit
  • 🔋 Leisure battery not charging
  • ⚡ Low or unstable charging voltage
  • 🔥 Overheating or burning smell
  • 🔌 Blown fuse or short circuit
  • 🛠 Damaged transformer or power components
  • ⚠ Intermittent charging operation
  • 📉 Battery voltage dropping under load
