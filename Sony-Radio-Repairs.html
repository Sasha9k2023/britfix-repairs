Specialist repairs for classic Sony world band and shortwave radios

Specialist Sony Radio Repair Service

At Britfix Repairs, we specialise in repairing classic Sony shortwave, world band and portable radio receivers at component level.

Many of these radios are affected by ageing or leaking electrolytic capacitors. These faults can cause distorted audio, low sound, intermittent operation, display problems or complete failure.

We can replace defective capacitors, repair damaged circuit board areas and install LED backlighting where the original display illumination has failed.

Electrolytic Capacitor Replacement

Leaking electrolytic capacitors are a common problem in several classic Sony radio models. The leaked material can affect nearby components and damage tracks or pads on the circuit board.

Our capacitor repair service can include:

  • Removal of defective electrolytic capacitors
  • Cleaning of affected circuit board areas
  • Repair of damaged tracks and solder pads where possible
  • Installation of quality replacement capacitors
  • Functional testing after repair

LED Backlight Upgrade

Some Sony radios suffer from weak or completely failed display illumination. Where suitable, we can replace or convert the original backlight to LED illumination.

An LED conversion can provide a brighter, more reliable and longer-lasting display backlight.

Component-Level Radio Repairs

We diagnose and repair faults at component level rather than replacing the whole radio. Repairs may include power supply, audio, control, display and backlight circuits.

Because these are older and sometimes rare radios, repair results depend on the condition of the circuit boards, availability of parts and any previous repair attempts.

UK Postal Repair Service

Customers throughout the UK can send their Sony radio to Britfix Repairs for inspection and repair.

Please contact us before posting your radio. Describe the model, fault symptoms and any previous repair work. We will then provide packing and delivery instructions.
